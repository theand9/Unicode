Features that can be included in the app in order to improve its functionality and viability are:
a] Functionality Aspect
    1) Categorization of Expenses. Eg. Food, Travel, Books etc
    2) Total Monthly Budgeting
    3) Savings and goal tracking
    4) Spending pattern tracker
    5) Data logging with options for pictures of bills and receipts
    6) Connection with digital wallet for a wider range of tracking
    7) Insights on places where money could be saved with alerts(point 4)
    8) Login for user(obvious)
    9) OPTIONAL: Student Loan tracker and amount of money to be saved to clear out the debt

b] Design Aspect:
    1) Card like interface
    2) Dashboard and Graphs showing expenses for an intuitive experience
    3) Flexibility of customizing graphs. User can customize graphs according to his/her choice
    4) On Demand Reports and Insights of spending pattern
